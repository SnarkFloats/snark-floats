Welcome to Snark Floats

I’m not here to hold your hand. This is the place where I drag my own half-drowned thoughts onto a life raft made of sarcasm, sharp humor, and stubborn honesty — and if you want to climb aboard, you’re welcome.

This isn’t a public service. It’s survival. I call bullshit where I see it — on bad tech, bad storytelling, bad history, corporate evil, and the small stupid things that try to sink us all.

Life’s a mess. Snark floats. Grab a seat if you can handle it. If not, there are plenty of other lifeboats out there, filled with Pinterest quotes and toxic positivity. Good luck. You’ll need it…
