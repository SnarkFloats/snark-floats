Welcome to Snark Floats

I’m not here to hold your hand. This is the place where I drag my own half-drowned thoughts onto a life raft made of sarcasm, sharp humor, and stubborn honesty. And if you want to climb aboard, you’re welcome. This ain't the Titanic, and I'm not hogging the door.

This isn’t a public service. It’s survival. My survival. I call bullshit where I see it—on bad tech, bad storytelling, bad history, corporate evil, and the small stupid things that try to sink us one annoyance at a time.

Life’s a mess. Snark floats.
Grab a seat if you can handle it. If not, there are plenty of other lifeboats out there padded with Pinterest quotes and toxic positivity. Good luck. You’ll need it.
