Snarklevision Presents... The Mass Effect Dream Cast

Main Characters

Commander Shepard – Dichen Lachman

Commanding, emotionally layered, quietly devastating presence. Hot as hell.
Because my Shepard is ALWAYS female. Sorry dudes.

Kaidan Alenko – Steven Yeun

Grounded, sensitive, morally centered. He carried TWD for 6 seasons with his warmth, and I just know he can warm up space, too.

Ashley Williams – Jessica Henwick

Military tough, potential for real growth. I don't always love Ashley, but I think I could if she were played by Jessica.

Garrus Vakarian – Jensen Ackles

Snarky, loyal, emotional backbone, sniper energy. And I suspect he could even make a blue alien with mandibles sexy.

Urdnot Wrex – Idris Elba

Voice of a god, presence of kings, tired warlord energy and RAGE. I mean... duh?

Tali'Zorah – Kristen Bell

Charming, emotionally rich, quirky but brave — the perfect blend of warmth and wit. I struggled to find anyone else who might even compete for the role.

Liara T'Soni – Dakota Fanning

Cerebral, vulnerable, terrifying when unleashed. She can go from naive bookworm to galaxy-spanning intel wizard believably.

Jeff "Joker" Moreau – Robert Sheehan

Sarcastic, fast-talking, emotionally sincere under layers of snark. And weirdly likable despite all that.

Admiral Anderson – Viola Davis

Gravitas, mentorship, emotional foundation, and strength. The first of my gender swaps, and there aren't many. But this one sang to me.

Dr. Karin Chakwas – Diane Lane

Steady, warm, world-weary caregiver. Chakwas is an emotional touchstone in most of my playthroughs, and Diane Lane can definitely pull that off.

Donnel Udina – Giancarlo Esposito

Slippery, smug, political creature you love to hate. Or maybe just hate. Either way, Giancarlo can NAIL him.

EDI – Jodie Comer

Evolving as we watch, dry wit, emotion emerging from logic. This will be a tough role to play, but I think Jodie can pull it off.

Miranda Lawson – Emily Blunt

Cool competence, icy exterior with hidden emotional depth. Many people wanted Emily for Shepard, and I could've gotten behind that. But I think this is the role where she'd really shine.

Jacob Taylor – David Ajala

Earnest, strong, principled, heart in the right place... Behind his abs. I think he'd make Jacob more lovable. Plus he has melty eyes to die for.

Mordin Solus – Doug Jones

Physically expressive, voice-flexible, deeply tragic. It was kinda a no-brainer. Did you see him in Star Trek: Discovery?

Samara / Morinth – Angela Bassett

Justicar dignity, lethal calm, stoic power wrapped in hotness. I just don't think this kind of feminine power could be played by just anyone.

Jack – Saoirse Ronan

Trauma manifest, chaotic, emotionally raw and dangerous. I wanted someone who could be the walking chaos and rage that is Jack but still be likable.

Thane Krios – Dev Patel

Poetic, spiritual, deadly, a penitent soul in conflict. Who can be all of that and still come across as kinda sexy?

Grunt – Alan Ritchson

Literally bred to fight, over-the-top, sweet rage monster. I mean, that's kinda a description of Ritchson as Reacher, isn't it?

Legion – Stanley Tucci

Learning to be a person, restrained wonder, unexpected depth. He can show us the tin man becoming real with only a few words.

Zaeed Massani – Karl Urban

Veteran merc, feral dad energy, grizzled and hilarious. This one might be a tad controversial, but I see Zaeed as an older space version of Urban's character in The Boys.

Kasumi Goto – Karen Fukuhara

Playful, stealthy, deadly, rogue energy with heart and a dash of heartbreak. And cute as a button, to boot!

The Illusive Man – Keanu Reeves

Cold charisma, tragic idealism, chilling delivery. This role begs for someone likable, because his intentions are good even if his methods are awful.

Harbinger – Shohreh Aghdashloo

Cosmic menace. Existential dread. Voice like annihilation wrapped in velvet fire. Her voice deserves a bigger part than Admiral Shala'Ran, so I picked one.

James Vega – Kevin Alejandro

Big softie with soldier’s heart, loyal and capable. This was a tough one, but I followed my gut on someone I thought could help James be more than he was in the game.

Aria T'Loak – Michelle Rodriguez

Regal ruthlessness, queen of Omega. Hell, she IS Omega. She has to be sexy and unquestionably badass. That pretty much defines Michelle Rodriguez.

Javik – Nonso Anozie

Ancient strength, commanding presence, disdain turned alliance. Anozie's deep voice, accent and presence seem perfection for this role.

ME1 Supporting Cast

Dr. Chloe Michel – Mélanie Laurent

Calm, competent, trusted. The Normandy’s medbay is in excellent hands if Dr. Chakwas isn't available for... some reason.

Matriarch Benezia – Gina Torres

Commanding, elegant, terrifying when cornered; mother, zealot, symbol. Benezia has to wear a lot of hats, and be beautiful and statuesque in all of them.

Nihlus – Sendhil Ramamurthy

Honorable, intense, mentor energy gone in a tragic flash. Casting Sendhil feels almost like a waste, but he would slay in the role.

Shaira (The Consort) – Claudia Black

Enigmatic, sensual, wise, with a voice that disarms and devastates. I love her voice, and it's a different role than she acted in the game, so it doesn't break the rules!

Khalisah Bint Sinan Al-Jilani – Gabrielle Union

Sharp, persistent, and fearless — too good at her job for anyone’s comfort

Chief Engineer Greg Adams – Josh Brolin

Gruff warmth, capable veteran, zero tolerance for BS. And believable as friendly to just about everyone.

Navigator Charles Pressly – Colin Firth

Stiff formality hiding a dry wit and eventual loyalty.

Gianna Parasini – Zendaya

Corporate investigator with a smile that says, "I’m absolutely arresting you later."

Emily Wong – Awkwafina

Relentless, quirky, charming pain in every government’s ass. I overcame my desire to avoid casting any actor with a single name for this one.

Barla Von – John Goodman

Grumbly gravitas in a tiny pressure suit — equal parts menace and comic relief.

Captain Kirrahe – Peter Dinklage

Vaguely ridiculous deadpan with real inspiration — "Hold the line!" Not many actors can play serious and ridiculous at the same time.

Rachni Queen – Kathleen Turner

Ancient alien intelligence with rich, resonant power and eerie calm. The sound of this one is everything.

Admiral Steven Hackett – Denzel Washington

Weight of command, with a voice that makes you want to follow orders. Must have presence out the wazoo.

ME2 Supporting Cast

Shadow Broker – Djimon Hounsou

Towering power cloaked in silence and strategy. And that voice...

Armando Owen-Bailey – Brian Cox

Sardonic authority, exhausted competence. He has to exude capability in equal parts with exasperation and disgust.

Kelly Chambers – Amanda Schull

Sweet and sincere, but underestimated. This odd role requires an actor who can pull of vaguely bland and traumatized equally well.

Gavin Archer – Gary Cole

Haunted genius, remorse buried under duty, heartbreaking in his fall. Broken priorities.

David Archer – Tom Cavanagh

Fragile, recovering, strange and deeply human. Powerful and terrifying but still sympathetic. That's a tough one.

Kal'Reegar – Pedro Pascal

Tough, battle-tested, deadpan with hidden warmth. You believe him as a badass marine who can still make you smile.

Veetor'Nara – Ben Whishaw

Traumatized, twitchy, sensitive, slowly rebuilding. Able to convey broken and recovering without showing any facial expressions.

Ronald Taylor – Dennis Haysbert

Cruel charisma, tragic delusion, menace that smiles. And believable as a dad to one of the main characters.

Donovan Hock – Mark Strong

Cultured and cruel, criminal flair with brute menace when it's called for.

Tela Vasir – Lashana Lynch

Relentless, skilled, ruthless with style. One of the tougher fights in ME2, so she had to be played by someone believably badass.

Feron – David Giuntoli

Shadowy ally, fierce loyalty, rogue charm. He's bent but not broken.

Kolyat Krios – Mason Gooding

Wounded son with a soul in conflict; searching for direction.

Detective Anaya – Hailee Steinfeld

Asari C-Sec officer, skeptical and sharp, respectful and dutiful. Ideally stands up even standing next to Samara.

Aresh Aghdashloo – Kirk Acevedo

Survivor of trauma, twitchy brilliance, edge of menace. Able to look Jack in the eye. For a second or two...

Warlord Okeer – Jimmy Smits

Ruthless vision of legacy, gravitas with broken ideals. Father of a thousand "failures" and one success.

Admiral Zaal'Koris vas Qwib-Qwib – Chiwetel Ejiofor

Idealistic dissenter, morally driven voice amid conflict. Honestly not terribly likable at first, but Ejiofor can make him grow on you the way he does in game.

Admiral Han'Gerrel vas Neema – Lee Pace

Aggressive posture, deep loyalty, charismatic under fire. Not afraid to sacrifice allies to win the war. Kind of a dick.

Admiral Shala’Raan vas Tonbay – Alfre Woodard

Maternal authority, moral strength, gravitas and compassion. Voiced by Shohreh Aghdashloo in the game; this is a tough one to live up to, but Alfre Woodard is up to the challenge.

Admiral Daro'Xen vas Moreh – Michelle Pfeiffer

Brilliant and cool, calculating with charm. Needs to convey superiority and intelligence just through voice.

Collector General – Ron Perlman

Voice of ancient horror, evolutionary zealot, alien intellect incarnate. Perlman's deep gravely voice with a little extra SFX will rock this.

ME3 Supporting Cast

Steve Cortez – Harry Shum Jr.

Grounded, grieving, kind. His strength is visible even in his quietness.

Eve (a.k.a. Urdnot Bakara) – Michelle Yeoh

Regal endurance, survivor strength, soft voice with steel underneath. She has to have the presence to represent the future of an entire race.

Samantha Traynor – Arden Cho

Nerdy and flirty with backbone and great teeth.

Dr. Garret Bryson – Edward Norton

Curious, obsessive, unshaven truth-hunter desperate for answers. Guess you should be careful what you wish for.

Dr. Ann Bryson – Ruth Wilson

Haunted, brilliant, emotionally raw. She picks up her father’s torch amid galactic chaos and manages to stand tall despite being a Leviathan meat puppet.

Nyreen Kandros – Danai Gurira

Fierce leadership, militant compassion, Omega's hope in a suit of armor. Honestly feels a lot like her role in the Marvel movies.

Diana Allers – Morena Baccarin

Smart, self-assured, can sell poise under fire while delivering a subtle middle finger and looking friggin' hot as hell doing it. Diana's a bad punchline in the game, but she won't be in my live action.

Matriarch Aethyta – Jamie Lee Curtis

Bartender, blunt asari “dad" with deep wells of pain and love buried under the sarcasm.

Oleg Petrovsky – Mads Mikkelsen

Cold strategist, elegant menace, burdened loyalty. If you threw cannibalism into that short description, it'd be Hannibal.

Brynn Cole – Grace Park

Smart, empathetic, quietly courageous. Has to be a believable match for Jacob.

Kai Leng – Daniel Dae Kim

Deadly precision, icy calm, intimidating presence. This casting is more about who I think Kai Leng SHOULD be, not who he is in the games.

Maya Brooks – Tatiana Maslany

Master of masks — bumbling analyst to deadly infiltrator without missing a beat. Obvious choice.

Primarch Victus – Samuel L. Jackson

Commanding presence, strategic mind, a voice you believe in under fire. He's got to stand against Wrex and Shepard and feel like he belongs there.

Tarquin Victus – Tom Hopper

Idealistic and impulsive, the son trying to honor a legacy. He needs to be clearly not as strong as his father, but trying very hard.

Dalatrass Linron – Helen Mirren

Right, politically shrewd and stuck defending a crumbling legacy in front of a firing squad. Also has to stand against Wrex, Shepard and Victus without any facial expression, so it had to be someone who oozes gravitas.
